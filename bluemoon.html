There are two types of Blue Moons but unfortunately, neither has anything to do with color.
A seasonal Blue Moon is the traditional definition of a Blue Moon and refers to the third full moon
in a season that has four full moons according to NASA.
Whilst the second definition — borne out of a misunderstanding of the first
— is a monthly Blue Moon which refers to the second full moon within a
single calendar month.
The monthly Blue Moon is nowadays considered the
second definition of a Blue Moon rather than a mistake,
